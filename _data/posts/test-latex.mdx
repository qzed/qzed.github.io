You can insert some $\LaTeX$ formula $f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^\infty \hat f(\xi)\,e^{2 \pi i \xi x} \,d\xi$ in the middle of a sentence using $\KaTeX$.
You can also put large formulas in their own paragraph.

$$
f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^\infty \hat f(\xi)\,e^{2 \pi i \xi x} \,d\xi
$$
